Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

Cashflow Control ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use the Cashflow Control mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password. We use this information to authenticate your account and enable cloud sync.

Financial Data You Enter. The App allows you to enter financial information such as account balances, transaction amounts, recurring payment schedules, and credit card details (balances, interest rates, minimum payments). This data is entered voluntarily by you to power the App's cashflow projections and debt payoff features.

Usage Analytics. We collect anonymized usage data to understand how the App is used and to improve the experience. This includes which features you use, screen views, and general interaction patterns. We do not tie analytics data to your financial information.

Crash and Performance Data. We collect crash reports and performance metrics to identify and fix bugs. This data may include device model, operating system version, and the state of the App at the time of a crash.

Subscription Information. If you subscribe, your payment is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but never see or store your payment method details.

2. How We Store Your Data

Local Storage. Your financial data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data remains on your device and is accessible only to the App.

Cloud Storage. If you create an account and enable sync, your financial data is encrypted in transit and stored securely on our cloud infrastructure powered by Supabase. Cloud storage allows you to back up your data and sync across devices.

Security. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) for all data transmitted between the App and our servers. Your data is stored in secure, access-controlled databases.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase — Authentication and cloud database storage. Supabase processes your email address and synced financial data. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. RevenueCat processes your anonymous user identifier and subscription status. It does not have access to your financial data. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • PostHog — Product analytics. PostHog receives anonymized usage events. It does not receive your financial data. PostHog Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — Error and crash reporting. Sentry receives crash data and device diagnostics. It does not receive your financial data. Sentry Privacy Policy

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's features
  • Authenticate your account and enable cloud sync
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the user experience
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs
  • Communicate with you about your account or service changes

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We share data only with the service providers listed in Section 3, and only to the extent necessary to operate the App. Each provider is bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or a government request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access. You can view all the financial data you have entered in the App at any time. You can also export your complete data set as a JSON file from the Settings screen.
  • Deletion. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@cashflowcontrol.app. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
  • Data Portability. You can export your data at any time using the App's built-in export feature.
  • Correction. You can edit or update any financial data you have entered directly within the App.

7. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@cashflowcontrol.app.

8. European Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including:

  • The right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Our legal basis for processing your data is your consent (when you create an account) and the performance of our contract with you (providing the App's services).

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your data from our servers within 30 days. Anonymized analytics data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and notify you through the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
