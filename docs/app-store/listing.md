## Description
Take control of your financial future with Cashflow Control — the smart cashflow tracker that helps you see where every dollar goes and find the fastest path out of debt.

**See Your Future Balance**
Project your bank balance up to 45 days ahead. Know exactly when bills hit and never be surprised by overdrafts again. Track income, expenses, and recurring payments in one clean view.

**Crush Your Debt**
Compare three proven payoff strategies — Avalanche, Snowball, and Minimum Payments — to find the fastest, cheapest path to being debt-free. See exactly how much interest you'll save with each approach.

**Smart Features**
- 45-day cashflow projections with interactive chart
- Track credit card debt with APR, balances, and payoff timelines
- Compare debt payoff strategies side-by-side
- Cloud sync across all your devices
- Dark mode support
- Export your data anytime

**Privacy First**
Your financial data is encrypted and synced securely. We never sell your information.

## App Review Notes
This app uses auto-renewable subscriptions:
- Monthly subscription: $4.99/month with 7-day free trial
- Subscription can be managed and cancelled in App Store Settings
- Payment is charged to iTunes account at confirmation of purchase
- Subscription automatically renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before end of current period
- Account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to end of current period

Demo: The app works in local-only mode without sign-in for the trial period. All features are available during the free trial.
